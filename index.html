Gender Inequality Index

The Gender Inequality Index (GII) reflects gender-based disadvantages in three dimensions—reproductive health, empowerment and the labour market—for as many countries as data of reasonable quality allow.
It is also possible to analyze time trend for each factor just clicking on a country.

The index (related to 2013 data) shows the loss in potential human development due to inequality between female and male achievements in these dimensions. It varies between 0, where women and men fare equally, and 1, where either gender fares as poorly as possible in all measured dimensions.
